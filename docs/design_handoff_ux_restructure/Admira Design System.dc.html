The system

Fit and honest chance, never collapsed.

Admira answers two separate questions for every school — is it a good fit, and what is the honest admissions range. The system leads with a confident verdict, then keeps the rigor one tap away. Oxide is chance. Teal is fit. Indigo is the reference. We name what we can't see.

01

Range, never a point

Every chance is an interval with a most-likely tick. We refuse false precision — the point is only a marker inside the band.

02

Fit ≠ admit odds

FIT is a 0–100 profile overlap shown beside the range. "Great fit, long odds" is a valid, complete answer.

03

Name what we can't see

Essays, recs and interest aren't in the model. We surface them as honest, direction-only levers — never a fabricated number.

18

Honesty & safety system

These behaviors are load-bearing, not decoration — every surface inherits them.

◍ Sample labeling

No hardcoded/decorative numbers as real reads. Marketing and demo figures are tagged illustration; real numbers come only from the module/data layer.

k-anonymity

Cohorts suppress below 5 consented records, with an honest empty state and provenance counts when shown.

Owner-RLS & consent

Profile and documents are owner-only; contributing to cohorts is explicit opt-in. No path bypasses consent.

Direction-only levers

Numeric deltas come only from re-running the model. Essays/recs/interest get direction, never a fabricated number.

Merit, not predicted

Money/ROI is deferred — a labeled stub with no dollar figures until the data is honest.

No ghostwriting

Narrative Studio surfaces and outlines themes; the words stay the student's. Copilot cites tool receipts and refuses to invent figures.

19

Voice

One voice, applied everywhere: lead with the confident verdict, keep rigor in methodology and tooltips — not as hedging headlines. The range, drivers and limits are always one tap away, never the opening line.

✓ Do
A target. Strong profile, fierce field.

Verdict first; the 24–38% range and drivers sit just below.

✕ Avoid
"Remember: ranges, not points. FIT is not admission chance. Read the range first…"

Hedging in the headline. Keep rigor in tooltips, not the lede.

Workflow journey

The smooth path through the product. One shared profile threads every step — set once, read everywhere — so the split never feels like ten apps. Copilot is available at every step.

1

Land & understand

Marketing hero states the two-question promise with a clearly-labeled sample read. One CTA: Get your read.

2

Set the profile the gate

Profile Studio captures academics and renders the fit radar. Saved under RLS, it becomes the single source every later page reads — no re-entry.

3

Land on the dashboard

At-a-glance verdict, list balance, next Climb move, Command progress. Each card links into its full module.

4

Explore schools & read deeply

Browse the Universe; open a school for the split-verdict read — range, radar, drivers, requirements, outcomes, similar programs.

5

Build a balanced list

Smart List spans reach/target/likely from real reads; Students-Like-You adds honest, k-safe context where it exists.

6

Improve & execute

Climb ranks the next score move; Command Center turns the list into tasks, deadlines and a document vault.

7

Tell the story & package

Narrative Studio and Compass shape the application; Reports assemble everything into a shareable, token-scoped export.
