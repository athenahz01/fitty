How the read is built

Methodology.

What the model sees
  • GPA & submitted test scores vs. published middle-50 bands
  • School selectivity tier from the Common Data Set
  • Application round (ED/RD) where the rate spread is published
  • Intended-major embedding for fit — separate from chance
What it does not
  • Essays and application narrative
  • Recommendations & demonstrated interest
  • Anything we'd have to invent a number for

Merit, not predicted dollars. Range first. We name what we can't see — and refuse false precision.

Privacy & consent

Your data, on your terms.

Honesty & safety
  • Owner-RLS: your profile and documents are readable only by you.
  • k-anonymity: cohorts suppress below 5 consented records.
  • Consent gating: nothing enters a shared cohort without opt-in.
  • No PII in logs or bundle.
